Things to be taken care of

  • Select a place where there is sufficient lighting.
  • Do not take photo with flash.
  • Do not apply any filters to the image.

How to capture the image

Image of eyes (containing palpebral conjunctiva)

Using mobile camera
  • Place one of your fingers under your lower eyelid and gently drag it down.
  • Using rear camera, ask someone to hold the phone such that the palpebral conjunctiva (Red colour part of lower eyelid) is clearly visible and then capture the image.
  • If using rear camera is not possible, you can capture the image with the front camera.
  • Repeat the same process and capture the image of other eye also.
  • Crop the images such that they look like below pictures.

Image of fingernails

  • Curl your fingers in a line and with the other hand capture the image.
  • Crop the image such that it looks like below picture.
